Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, UPI, and net banking. We also offer cash on delivery for orders above ₹1000.

How long does shipping take?

+

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within India. Express shipping is available for next-day delivery in select cities.

What is your return policy?

+

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unused items with original tags. Custom orders are not eligible for returns.

Do you offer customization?

+

Yes, we offer customization services for most of our products. Please contact our customer service for more details.
